ApiaryAI offers a one-time in-app purchase to unlock all premium features. All purchases are processed through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store and are subject to the refund policies of those platforms.

Apple App Store Refunds

If you made your purchase through the Apple App Store, you must request a refund directly from Apple. Apple handles all transactions and refund requests independently of ApiaryAI.

To request a refund:

  • Visit https://reportaproblem.apple.com
  • Sign in with your Apple ID
  • Locate the purchase and follow the instructions to request a refund

Google Play Store Refunds

If you made your purchase through Google Play:

  • You may be eligible for a refund if requested within 48 hours
  • Visit https://play.google.com/store/account
  • Click "Order History"
  • Locate the item and choose "Request a refund"

Google processes all payments and refunds on their own terms.

No Direct Refunds

As a developer, we do not have access to your payment information or the ability to issue direct refunds. All requests must be handled through Apple or Google per their respective policies.
